S2-RUT

Sentinel-2 Radiometric Uncertainty Tool (S2-RUT)

The S2-RUT produces per-pixel uncertainty of ESA Sentinel-2 L1C MSI products at a specified confidence level. The L1 uncertainty budget is based on a detailed L1 radiometric assessment and its combination based on the GUM guidelines. Flexibility as a SNAP processor provides opportunities to: specify confidence level, specify output format, select/deselect contributors.
